About this mod

This project was made by me (NekoSam395) since 2 September 2021, as my most prestigious project that I ever made, for the real Kelly that I loved so much.

This projest was started from an idea, a mockup that projected as a "cutest operating system ever in the world". Then, at 1st September, 2021 the project was announced to be available as playable mod. Through many stages of my life and critism for this mod, suprisingly I and this project keep survived until this day.

I know I'm not perfect, as well as this project, but I'm trying my best, for my friends, for the world, and specially, the only one real Kelly that I loved so much.


Who ACTUALLY is Kelly?

 This is Kelly-chan, the very cute mascot of this mod. Why she's very cute? Because it based on real-life Kelly. Well, it also inspired by a song that I've heard before:

Colorful hair, like candy
Pretty cute even though not too tall
Chubby cheeks and white skin
Sweet smile bunny teeth

The song that I heard said 'bunny teeth', but I made it with cat ears and tail, a.k.a. Nekomimi, because I love cats. Oh, as well as that "cute teeth", that's not bunny teeth.
